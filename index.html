DI(C)E is an exhibition showcasing the endless possibilities of random generation through the lens of the most well-known table top game, Dungeons & Dragons. Through the usage of randomly generated outcomes, numbers, and images, viewers will get a chance to go on their own adventure, however long or short it may be.

A note to all adventurers: upon receiving your outcomes, please click on the print button to export them as PDFs. Then click on the save button to save it into the Google drive folder.
