DI(C)E is an exhibition showcasing the endless possibilities of random generation through the lens of the most well-known table top game, Dungeons & Dragons. Through the usage of randomly generated outcomes and images, users will get a chance to go on their own adventure, however long or short it may be.

A note to all adventurers: upon receiving your outcome, please click on the print button to export it as a PDF. Then click on the save button to save it into the Google drive folder. Please repeat for all outcomes generated.
